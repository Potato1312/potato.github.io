DIPLOMA IN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (R/482/4/0213)

MQA/FA 1475 (R/482/4/0213)

This programme is designed to equip students who want to understand information systems from a technological perspective, the organizational contexts in which these systems are developed and the business environments which determine how the systems can be used to create value. Curriculum, course content, academic standards and course evaluation are specifically designed to enable students to gain and develop the skills and knowledge relevant for a career in Information Technology.


OBJECTIVES

Upon the completion of this programme, students should be able to:

  • Understand the basic principles of IT and Technology
  • Apply various types of computing skills needed to function at an optimum level
  • Demonstrate concepts and technologies in practical situations to develop effective programming, software development and research solutions
  • Apply relevant techniques related to IT and programming and function well in organization

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Graduates of Diploma in Business Administration programme will have the opportunity to further their studies in Bachelor Degree in related fields at local universities, and are qualified to apply for the positions below:

  • IT Assistant Executive
  • Network Support Engineer
  • Software engineer
  • Software/ Web Programmer
  • Information System Analyst
